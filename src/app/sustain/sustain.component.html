Sustainability

We employ diverse strategies to reduce our environmental footprint, conserve resources, and promote the long-term sustainability of our operations, aligning with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. Our ongoing efforts drive progress toward environmentally friendly and socially responsible aquaculture practices.

Energy Efficiency

We reduce energy consumption by implementing energy-efficient lighting, heating, ventilation, and cooling systems. Installing energy-efficient equipment and appliances, such as high-efficiency pumps and motors. We utilize renewable energy sources like solar panels to power the hatchery.

Water Conservation

By reducing water wastage, implementing efficient water use practices, and promoting responsible water management, we contribute to the preservation of clean and safe drinking water sources, protection of aquatic ecosystems, and the advancement of global access to this life-sustaining resource.

Waste Management

Routine planning to minimize and properly handle waste generated by the hatchery. Implement recycling programs for materials like plastic, paper, and glass. Compost organic waste and reuse it as fertilizer for landscaping or gardening.

Sustainable Feed

Feed for livestock is ensured that it is nutritionally balanced to support healthy growth and development of the hatchlings. Low environmental impact feed and minimal fishmeal or fish oil usage is encouraged.

Biosecurity

Robust biosecurity measures to minimize the risk of disease outbreaks. Strict protocols for equipment disinfection, livestock health and controlled access in the hatchery. This helps in reducing the need for antibiotics, promoting long-term sustainability.

Life Below Water

Unibio aims to conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development ensuring the the rich biodiversity, while also promoting the sustainable livelihoods of those who depend on these marine resources.

What's Next ?

While adding business value to our customers, we progress toward a more environmentally responsible and socially equitable business operation in our constant endeavor to ensure animal welfare and food safety.

Certifications

We are certified and compliant with internationally recognized sustainability standards like Best Aquaculture Practices (BAP) program of Global Seafood Alliance (GSA). BAP certification along with traceability information can help our farmers and the processing companies to provide further assurance to the consumers through their seafood buying and distribution companies.

Decision Making

We are implementing data collection and analysis systems not only in our hatcheries, but also to track our seed's performance in the farms in different environmental conditions. We use this data to make informed decisions and continuously aim to improve our culture practices.

Knowledge Sharing

Fostering collaboration with our farmers, research institutions, universities, and other hatcheries to share knowledge on best practices in sustainable hatchery operations. Also staying updated on the latest advancements in sustainable aquaculture techniques and incorporating them into our operations.
