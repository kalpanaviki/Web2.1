About UniBio

UniBio (India) Hatcheries is a leading supplier of good quality SPF Black Tiger shrimp (P. monodon) seed (post larvae) in India. The seeds are produced using SPF broodstock imported from Aqualma (Unima Group), Madagascar, one of the few commercially successful monodon domestication and SPF breeding programs in the world.

Our seed stocks are the outcome of three decades of proven maturation & seed production technology and continue to evolve keeping the farming success and the overall superior quality of the finished products in mind.

Vision

To be a trustworthy source for good quality SPF Monodon (Black Tiger Shrimp) seed.


Mission

To produce and supply genetically superior SPF monodon shrimp seeds that meet the highest quality standards resulting in sustainably profitable farming.
